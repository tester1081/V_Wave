Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and platform.

How do I create an account on VeltraWave?
Creating an account on VeltraWave is simple. Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page, fill in your details, verify your email address, and you're good to go! You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for a quicker process.
How long does it take for services to be delivered?
Most of our services are delivered instantly after payment confirmation. Data plans, airtime, and cable TV subscriptions are typically activated within seconds. However, some services like Free Fire top-ups may take up to 30 minutes as they are processed manually.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, card payments, and payments through your VeltraWave wallet. You can fund your wallet using any of these methods and then use the wallet balance for quick transactions.
How do I report an issue with my order?
If you experience any issues with your order, you can contact our support team through the "Contact Us" page, send an email to support@veltrawave.com, or reach out via WhatsApp at +234 706 747 9043. Please provide your order ID and a description of the issue for faster resolution.
Do you offer refunds for failed transactions?
Yes, we offer refunds for failed transactions. If your transaction fails and you're charged, please contact our support team with your transaction details. We'll investigate and process a refund to your VeltraWave wallet or original payment method within 24-48 hours.
